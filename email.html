UDYAMI 2021

Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Panjab University successfully organised it's 4th edition of annual national level Entrepreneurship-Summit of Panjab University. It is one of the finest E-Summits in India. The 2-day extravaganza witnessed 400 students and professionals from the industry to promote entrepreneurship. This year's theme was "Startup Spectrum", in which we conducted 7 talk sessions on 7 different startup domains like music, travel, etc. Four competitions and our flagship event Startup of the Event received immense appreciation from the audience.
